The Best Movies According to Jermaine

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A Silent Voice

story of a former bully turned social outcast, who decides to reconnect and befriend the deaf girl he had bullied years prior.

The Matrix

Really cool sci-fi movie.

The Pianist

Polish musician Wladyslaw faces various struggles as he loses contact with his family. As the situation worsens, he hides in the ruins of Warsaw in order to survive.
